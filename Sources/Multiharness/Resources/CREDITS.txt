agent-ding.wav
==============

A two-tone descending airplane-style chime ("bing-bong", A5 → E5) generated
programmatically by scripts/generate-ding.py. The script renders pure sine
waves with bell-like exponential decay envelopes to 16-bit PCM.

License: none required. Pure synthesized sine waves do not carry a copyright;
the audio data is a deterministic function of the script. The script itself
is part of this repository and licensed under the same terms as the rest of
Multiharness.

To regenerate (e.g. after tweaking tones, duration, or envelope), run:

    python3 scripts/generate-ding.py

It writes to Sources/Multiharness/Resources/agent-ding.wav.
